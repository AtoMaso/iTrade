Help

Please provide all required information needed (marked in red) to register to the application.
  • The name should contain your first and second name only and with maximum of 50 characters in length.
  • Your username should be your ATONET account username.
  • Your email will be your ATO emaill account.The application will not accept domain other then "ato.gov.au".
  • The application will allow email of 70 characters in length".
  • Your password SHOULD NOT be your ATONET account password.
  • The password should be between 6 and 10 character in length and should contain at least one numeric character and one upper case character.
An account with the application author's permissions containing your credentials has been created. As an author you will able to add articles and remove your articles from the application. To add an articles select "Add New" button on the All Articles view. Please use the "Login" button to log into the application with your newly created credentials.
